Privacy Policy

Never Miss A Call — last updated July 23, 2026

Never Miss A Call ("we," "us") provides an AI phone answering service for small home-services businesses. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we handle mobile phone numbers and text messages.

We do not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information collected for text messaging is used solely to deliver the service described below.

Information we collect

  • Business owner information: name, business name, mobile phone number, and business details (services, hours, service area) provided when signing up for our service.
  • Caller information: when our AI receptionist answers a call on behalf of a client business, we collect the caller's name, callback phone number, service address, and a description of their request, so the business owner can return the call.
  • Call metadata: time of call and originating phone number.

How we use information

  • To answer calls on behalf of client businesses and capture customer leads.
  • To send text message notifications of new leads to the business owner.
  • To display captured leads on a private dashboard for the business owner.
  • To provide support and improve the service.

Text messaging (SMS)

We send text messages for two purposes: (1) notifying business owners of new customer leads captured on their behalf, and (2) sending a demo phone number to business owners who verbally request it during a phone conversation with us.

  • Message frequency varies based on how many calls a business receives. Lead notifications are sent as calls come in.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP at any time to opt out of text messages. Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Mobile phone numbers used for text messaging are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

How we share information

We share information only as needed to operate the service:

  • With our telephony and AI service providers (such as Twilio and OpenAI) strictly to deliver calls, texts, and voice processing.
  • Caller lead details are shared only with the specific client business the caller was trying to reach.
  • When required by law.

We do not sell personal information.

Data retention and security

Lead records are retained so business owners can access their call history, and are deleted on request or when a client ends service. We use reasonable technical measures to protect stored information. Each client's leads are accessible only through their own private dashboard link.

Call recording

Our service does not record call audio by default. If recording is ever enabled for a client, callers will be notified at the start of the call as required by applicable state law.

Your choices

  • Opt out of text messages any time by replying STOP.
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information by contacting us.
